🏛️ About the Project

Advancing Bengali
Language AI

Bengali is spoken by over 230 million people, yet most large language models perform poorly on Bengali tasks. Our project at the Calcutta University Data Science Lab addresses this gap by fine-tuning the Qwen 2.5-3B model on carefully curated Bengali datasets.

We focus on reading comprehension, question answering, conversational fluency, and instruction-following — resulting in a model that understands Bengali significantly better than the base model.
